Simple by design

How It Works

1

Set Up a Trivia Tournament

Your foundation picks a category — club legends, match history, Premier League knowledge — and chooses a prize fans will compete for: a signed shirt, match tickets, or a stadium tour.

2

Your Fans Enter

Share the trivia link (or QR code) through social channels and supporter groups. Fans pay a small entry fee, play the game, and battle up the live leaderboard.

3

Community Wins

The top scorer wins the club-chosen prize. Every entry fee goes directly to your foundation and community projects. Fans get a brilliant experience — your cause gets the funding.

The smart fundraising choice

Why Trivia?

❤️

Fans Love to Compete

Football supporters are passionate and competitive. A club-themed trivia tournament channels that energy into fundraising — they give because they want to win.

🔗

Spreads Through Fan Networks

Tournaments spread organically through supporter groups, club socials, and WhatsApp threads. More entries, more money, more community impact.

🏅

Club-Chosen Prizes

Signed shirts, tickets, meet-and-greets, unique experiences — prizes only your club can offer, so fans can't get them anywhere else.

💷

Fully Transparent Fundraising

Every entry fee (minus a small management fee) goes directly to your foundation. Secure payments, funds released when the tournament closes.
