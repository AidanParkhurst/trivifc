How It Works

"We need to raise funds" to "Foundation just received a donation" in 3 steps.

01

Set Up a Trivia Tournament

Your foundation picks a category: club legends, match history, Premier League knowledge, UK history, etc. Then choose the prize fans will compete for: a signed shirt, match tickets, or a stadium tour.

02

Your Fans Enter

Promote the trivia link (QR code) to your fanbase through your club's social channels, supporter groups, etc. Fans pay an entry fee, play the game, and battle up the live leaderboard. The more you share, the more you raise.

03

Community Wins

The top scorers wins the club-chosen prize. Every entry fee goes directly to your foundation and your community projects. Fans get a brilliant, fun experience, and your cause gets the funding it deserves.

The smart fundraising choice

Why Trivia?

Because its fun! A new way to drive fan engagement, and raise money for your foundation.

Fans Love to Compete

Football supporters are already passionate about their team, and competitive. A club-themed trivia tournament channels that energy into fundraising. They give because they want to win, and support their community.

Spreads Through Fan Networks

The trivia tournament spreads organically through supporter groups, club social channels, and WhatsApp threads. More entries, more money, more impact for your community.

Club-Chosen Prizes

Tickets, merch, meet-and-greets, one of a kind experiences, etc. Prizes only your club can offer, so fans can't get them anywhere else.

Fundraise for Your Foundation

Every dollar of entry fees (minus management fee) goes directly to your foundation. Full transparency.
